4 reasons to choose Soup Culture

1 FRESH AND HEALTHY

Only natural ingredients - seasonal vegetables, local products and spices. Our soups does not contain meat, eggs, artificial flavor enhancers.

2 TASTE

The recipes for our cream soup are specially created for soup lovers - we gathered recipes from different parts of the World. The bread cup is a great compliment to the soup itself.

3 ECOLOGICAL

Our bread cup is a smart alternative to plastic. It does not pollute the environment and represents the idea of zero waste.

4 INNOVATIVENESS

No need for cutlery and you have all the mobility to walk around the city, enjoying soup on the go.
